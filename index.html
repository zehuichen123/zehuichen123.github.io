Biography

I am a 2nd-year master student at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), advised by Prof. Feng Zhao. I got a B.E. degree at Tongji University in 2020.

My research interests include object detection (2D, 3D, or both of them), instance segmentation, and unsupervised learning.
